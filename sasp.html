Ms. Saswati Roy (Deputy Registrar)

Introduction: The Administration Section of the Institute caters to the Non Academic employees of IIT Bombay i.e. Group A Officers, Group B & Group C employees. The core activities of the Section are divided into three segments:- Recruitment, Employee data management, Promotions according to the existing RR & PP, Career Advancement Scheme for Library Cadre, Dynamic Assured Career Progression Scheme for Doctors, financial upgradation under MACPS, Submission of statutory reports, Extension of temporary employment, Re-employment of staff and Officers, Establishment related maters (i.e. extending Service and Retirment Benefits to the employees), management of Group Term Insurance Policy, Performance Based Incentive Scheme, Scholarship awards to wards of employees etc.

    1) Recruitment-

    • Recruitment of non-faculty staff in Group A, B, C and erstwhile Group D(Compassionate ground appointees) Appointment on compassionate and sympathetic grounds.
    • Engagement of part-time medical officers, visiting specialists, and part-time coaches.
    • Maintenace of all Recruitment related and manpower related data Pay fixation on Recrutiment and Pay Protection Maintenance of MIS, dashboards, and institutional data.
    • Submission of statutory reports and correspondence with MoE/MHRD, Board of Governors, and other statutory bodies.
    • Providing administrative support for grievance redressal mechanisms.
    • Transfers

    2) Promotions –

    • Promotion and career advancement of non-faculty employees as per Recruitment Rules & Promotion Policy in force.
    • Implementation of Recruitment Rules & Promotion Policy (RR&PP) and revision of the same.
    • Extension of temporary employment Re-employment of staff and Officers Administering Career progression schemes such as DACP and CAS.
    • Financial Upgradation of employees through MACPS Pay fixation on account of Promotion

    3)  Establishment –

    • Maintenance and management of service records, service books, and personal files of Non-teaching staff Handling service-related matters such as confirmation, probation, promotion, Leave management etc.
    • Retirement related activities – Issuance of Retirement Notices, Pension Payment Orders, calling of No Dues Certificates, family pension claims etc.
    • Management of Group Term Insurance Policy, Management of Performance Based Incentive Scheme Administering the Diamond Jubilee Scholarship award, Prof. S.P. Sukhatme Award to wards of employees etc.
